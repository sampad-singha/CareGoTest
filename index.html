Security, Trust & Verification Framework

Your Safety is Our Top Priority

We follow a strict 3-layer verification and monitoring system to ensure complete peace of mind for you and your loved ones.

1. Strict Identity KYC

Both users and caregivers must complete real-time identity verification for maximum security.

  • ✓ Real-time selfie biometric validation
  • ✓ National ID (NID) verification & matching
  • ✓ KYC is mandatory before all bookings

2. Professional Screening

Every caregiver goes through a multi-step background and credential verification process.

  • ✓ Educational degree verification
  • ✓ Clinical registry & experience tracking
  • ✓ Reference & employment verification
  • ✓ Police clearance verification (optional)

3. Platform Accountability

Our technology and operations team monitor every service round-the-clock.

  • ✓ 24/7 admin dashboard monitoring
  • ✓ GPS check-ins for every visit
  • ✓ Emergency response & rapid support
  • ✓ Secure payment & cash safety thresholds
